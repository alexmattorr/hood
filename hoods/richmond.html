Richmond

Bordering Golden Gate Park to the south and the Presidio to the north, the Richmond District is an under-the-radar residential neighborhood surrounded by green space. Although it’s not the height of hipness, it’s important to note that the Richmond is not uncool. Instead, it’s a laid-back locale with simple and delicious dining options and friendly neighborhood bars. It takes time, but this west San Francisco neighborhood’s bus routes provide direct access to the city’s center.
